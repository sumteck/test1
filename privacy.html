Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Remake Bill Manager ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our web application to manage your SPARK and BiMS bills.

2. Use of Google User Data

Our application requires access to your Google Account (specifically Google Sheets API) to function properly. We use this access strictly for the following purposes:

  • To automatically create a spreadsheet named "Remake_Bills_Data" in your Google Drive.
  • To read and write your parsed bill data directly into this specific spreadsheet.

We do NOT read, access, modify, or delete any other files or spreadsheets in your Google Drive.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

All your data is stored directly in your personal Google account. We do not have any external databases, we do not store your data on our servers, and we do not collect any personal information in the background.

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

4. Security

Your data's security is guaranteed by Google's robust infrastructure, as the data never leaves your own Google Account ecosystem. Authentication is handled securely via Google OAuth 2.0.
